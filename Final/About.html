Welcome to Jugal Glass and Framing Center

At Jugal Glass and Framing Center, we are passionate about turning your memories into timeless pieces of art. With our expertise in glasswork and framing, we bring your visions to life. Our dedicated team ensures the highest quality craftsmanship, making each project unique and special.

Meet the visionary behind our success - Mr. Rajendra Gajurel, the owner and founder. His passion for art and commitment to excellence drive our team to deliver outstanding results.

Whether you're looking for custom framing, glass solutions, or personalized art pieces, Jugal Glass and Framing Center is your one-stop destination. Join us in creating memories that last a lifetime. With a legacy spanning over 30 years, Jugal Glass and Framing Center has been a beacon of quality and craftsmanship since its inception in the early 1990s. Our commitment to providing top-notch glass solutions and framing services has solidified our reputation as a trusted establishment in the industry. Our service speaks for itself.
